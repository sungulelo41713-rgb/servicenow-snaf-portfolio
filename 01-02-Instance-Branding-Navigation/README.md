Kasi Clean Energy Initiative
The Real-World Context:
With load-shedding and high electricity tariffs impacting small businesses across South African townships (like Soweto), a fictional non-profit enterprise called "Kasi Clean Energy" distributes subsidized solar panels and inverter kits to local spaza shops and community centers. 
They need a single, easily navigable platform structure to brand their initiative, manage logistics inquiries, and allow field operators to track installations.
